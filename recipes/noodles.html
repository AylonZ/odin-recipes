Noodles Teriyaki

Noodles Teriyaki is the most common dish in Japan the reason the noodles are so populer is giving by his simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of fine instant noodles
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 5 grams of ginger
  • 100 mill of Teriyaki souce

Optional Toppings

  • Peanuts
  • Sliced green onion
  • Sesame seads

Setp By Step

  1. Chope the vegteables
  2. Boil the noodles in hot water in a big pot
  3. Fried the vegteables in a wok pan
  4. Fried the noodles all together and add the Teriyaki souce
