How To Make Pasta Dough From Scratch

This recipe shows you how to make pasta dough by hand. The dough is perfect for rolling out so there is no pasta machine needed.

The Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons Water

Setp By Step Tutorial

  1. Make a mounten with the Flour
  2. box it with your hand and add the eggs
  3. scramble it with a fork and adds flour trough the process
  4. make a ball let it rest for 1 hour
  5. make any shape you like and boil it for 1-3 minutes
